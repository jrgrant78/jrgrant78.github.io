A FEW WORDS about the AUTEUR

J.R. Grant is a professional multimedia designer, filmmaker, and storyteller with over 15 years of experience working in film and video production.

During his career, he has cut together many human-interest films that have aired on PBS as well as numerous film festivals nationwide.

He has also garnered an award for best feature editing from the DocUtah International Film Festival, 2nd runner up documentary from the Royal Starr Film Festival, numerous official selections at other film festivals, and two Michigan Emmy nominations.

J.R. Grant has also attended numerous filmmaking workshops and seminars and has spent countless hours with his own independent studies in the area of film and video work in order to keep up with the current trends and continue to expand his knowledge in the evergrowing field of film and video production.
