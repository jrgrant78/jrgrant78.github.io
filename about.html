A FEW WORDS about the AUTEUR

The word 'Auteur' origintated from French film criticism of the late 1940s represented by a theory of filmmaking in which the director is viewed as the major creative force in a motion picture - the author of the film.

I am a professional multimedia designer, filmmaker, and storyteller with over 15 years of experience working in film and video production.

During my career, I have cut together many human-interest films that have aired on PBS as well as numerous film festivals nationwide.

I have also garnered an award for best feature editing from the DocUtah International Film Festival, 2nd runner up documentary from the Royal Starr Film Festival, numerous official selections at other film festivals, and two Michigan Emmy nominations.

I've also attended numerous filmmaking workshops and seminars and have spent countless hours with my own independent studies in the area of film and video work in order to keep up with the current trends and continue to expand upon my knowledge in the evergrowing field of film and video production.
