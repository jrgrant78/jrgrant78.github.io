    ABOUT

    I am a professional motion graphics designer, filmmaker, and storyteller with 20 years of experience working in film and video production. During my career, I have cut together many human-interest films that have aired on PBS and seen at numerous film festivals nationwide.

    I have also garnered an award for best feature editing from the DocUtah International Film Festival, 2nd runner up documentary from the Royal Starr Film Festival, numerous official selections at other film festivals, and multiple Michigan Emmy nominations.

    Check out my awards!

    I have worked with families to create lasting memories for their loved ones in the form of legacy documentaries, wedding films, and other celebratory events.

    I have worked with business leaders in the creation of corporate videos to help promote and sell their business.

    I have also attended numerous filmmaking workshops and seminars and have spent countless hours with my own independent studies in the area of film and video work in order to keep up with the current trends and continue to expand upon my knowledge in the evergrowing field of film and video production.
