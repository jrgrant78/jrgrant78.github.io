A FEW WORDS about the AUTEUR


J.R. Grant is a professional multimedia designer, filmmaker, and storyteller with over 15 years of experience working in film and video production.


Since the start of 2010 he has worked as senior editor, motionographer, and web designer for Visionalist Entertainment Productions - A documentary company producing human-interest films for Detroit Public Television.


While accruing numerous awards and nominations, he has secured his solid place in the ranks of film professionals.


J.R. Grant has attended numerous filmmaking workshops and seminars and has spent countless hours with his own independent studies in the area of film and video work along with numerous personal and professional level productions.
